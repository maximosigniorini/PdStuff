ElectroAcousticTools

EAT_Mexicanwave~

This module is a multi-point panner. It provides a mexican wave behaviour for 1-12 channels (13 in total including dedicated subwoofer channel 13).

Creation Arguments

EAT_Mexicanwave~ ID_tag n-channels

e.g. EAT_Mexicanwave~ Grill 12

Parameter

Gain: Control maximum gain in overall wave behaviour.
Gradient: Control wave shape from small and sharp to wide and gentle.
Frequency: Control speed and direction of mexican wave behaviour (stationary in middle).
ADV: Open 12x12 input mixer.
MIDI: Access MIDI learn and MIDI control on/off for parameter control.
HELP: Access help files.
